## 摘要
一些生物活性化合物对生物体的影响取决于其浓度和梯度，例如激素和信号肽，它们决定细胞身份、活性和生物体发育。目前有少量方法可以在植物上局部应用生化物质以产生空间上集中的浓度峰，例如琼脂块和显微注射，但这些方法缺乏精度、通量和/或简便性。我们开发了MicroTron，这是一种基于微流控的方法，专门用于丝状生物或生命阶段，如藓类植物小立碗藓的原丝体，作为在单细胞上应用化学物质并研究细胞响应的平台。我们展示了如何通过染料和激素在细胞侧面或顶端进行化学应用，靶向细胞壁、细胞膜、细胞质和细胞核。处理可以应用于单根丝，并在最佳条件下精度可达单个细胞。该方法可用于以高时空分辨率研究对化学物质的活体响应。

## Abstract
O_LIThe effect of some bioactive compounds on living organisms is dependent on their concentration and gradients, as is the case of hormones and signalling peptides, determining cell identity, activity and organism development.
C_LIO_LIThere are a handful of methods that allow to produce spatially confined peaks of concentration local application of biochemicals on plants, such as agar blocks and microinjection, but they lack in precision, throughput and/or simplicity.
C_LIO_LIWe developed the MicroTron, a microfluidics-based method specifically for filamentous organisms or life cycle stages, like the moss plant Physcomitrium patens protonemata, that serves as a platform for the application of chemicals on single cells and study the cell response.
C_LIO_LIWe show how chemical applications could be performed on cells, either on the side or apically with dyes and hormones, targeting the cell wall, cell membrane, cytosol and nucleus.
C_LIO_LITreatments could be applied on single filaments and with a precision of up to single cells in optimal conditions.
C_LIO_LIThis method could be used to study live responses to chemicals with high spatiotemporal resolution.
