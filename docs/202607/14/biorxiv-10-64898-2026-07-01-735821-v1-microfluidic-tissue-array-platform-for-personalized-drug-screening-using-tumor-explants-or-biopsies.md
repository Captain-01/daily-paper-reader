## 摘要
尽管乳腺癌分子特征有了重大改进，但当前的生物标志物在准确预测治疗效果方面仍显不足。对肿瘤组织进行离体检测，保持其天然构象，是指导个体患者治疗的直接策略，但面临挑战。在本研究中，我们利用小活检样本（<1mm³）开发了一种微流控组织阵列（FTA），模拟生理流动以实现营养物质和废物的持续交换，并保留肿瘤天然基质。将细胞/患者来源的乳腺癌异种移植组织在阵列中维持超过两周，并检测它们对治疗药物阿霉素或来那替尼的反应。在uFTA中，药物反应显示肿瘤细胞活力降低超过2倍，这与携带相同肿瘤负荷的小鼠肿瘤体积缩小相一致。EdU/Ki67检测表明，药物治疗后选择性保留了增殖能力较高的细胞，凸显了体内临床相关性。我们还开发了一种带阀门的FTA，以提高通量并在同一芯片上实现更多样化的治疗条件。总之，这种FTA可作为一种强大、低成本的台式诊疗工具，用于个性化癌症治疗，并符合FDA的新方法学要求。

## Abstract
Despite major improvements in molecular characterization of breast cancer, current biomarkers still fall short in accurate treatment prediction. Interrogating tumor tissue ex-vivo in its native conformation is a direct strategy for guiding treatment of individual patients but presents a challenge. In this study, we developed a microfluidic tissue array (FTA) using small biopsy samples (< 1mm3) mimicking physiological flow for consistent exchange of nutrients and waste, retaining the tumor native stroma. Cell/patient-derived breast cancer xenograft tissues were maintained over 2 weeks in the array and their response to therapeutic agents, doxorubicin or neratinib, were interrogated. Drug response in the uFTA showed >2-fold reduction in tumor cell viability which corroborated tumor size shrinkage in mice bearing the same tumor load. EdU/Ki67 assays indicated selective retention of cells with higher proliferative capacity after drug treatment, underscoring in vivo clinical relevance. We have also developed a valved-FTA to increase throughput and variety of treatment conditions on the same chip. Together, this FTA can be staged as a powerful, low-cost benchtop theranostic tool for personalized cancer therapeutics compatible with FDAs New Approach Methods.
